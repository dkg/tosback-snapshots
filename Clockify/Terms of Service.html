    As a User of Cloud Clockify, you may upgrade your plan at any time.       You are instantly being charged for the next billing period (a month or a year) and, after payment, you obtain immediate access to all Extra Features for the Paid Plan you choose based on the number of seats.

    • (2) Upgrading to a higher Paid Plan
      You are instantly being charged for the next Subscription Term on a pro rata basis and you obtain immediate access to all Extra Features for the higher plan. Your next billing date is after the expiry of the Subscription Term starting from the next day from the day payment has been made.

      You are instantly being charged for the next year on a pro rata basis. Your next billing date is after a year starting from the next day from the day payment has been made.

      We charge for the new seats on a pro rata basis for that billing period and the total (increased) number of seats from the next billing period.     As a Subscriber, you may downgrade your Paid Plan at any time. By downgrading your Paid Plan we mean either switching to a plan that offers less or no Extra Features and/ or switching to any monthly Paid Plan from an annual Paid Plan and/or reducing the number of seats.

      After your then-current Subscription Term expires, your access to the Extra Features will be denied and you can continue to use the Free Plan.

      After your then-current Subscription Term expires, your access to the Extra Features offered in the current Paid Plan will be denied, you will be charged for the lower Paid Plan, based on the Subscription Term you chose and you can continue to use the lower Paid Plan.

    • (4) Downgrading from the annual lower Paid Plan to the monthly higher Paid Plan
